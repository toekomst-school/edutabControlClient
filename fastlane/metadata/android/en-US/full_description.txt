EduTab Control - Device Management for Educational Tablets

* Quick and Easy to Install
* Compatible with Most Android Devices
* Manages Devices in School Networks
* Essential MDM Features for Education

EduTab Control helps schools manage their educational tablets efficiently. It's designed for managing classroom devices, replacing hours of maintenance work with a few clicks!

* Group-based policy management ensures all classroom devices work consistently.
* Device status monitoring makes it easy to identify and resolve issues quickly.
* Remote support simplifies IT work for schools.

EduTab Control connects to your EduTab server for device management.
